## 🚀 How to Use the Application

### For Users
1. Go to `/register` to create an account
2. Browse products at `/products` with filters
3. Click a product to see details and reviews
4. Add items to cart
5. View cart and checkout
6. Track orders at `/orders`
7. Manage profile at `/profile`
8. Add items to wishlist (heart icon)

### For Admin
1. Login at `/admin/login` with:
   - Email: `admin@gmail.com`
   - Password: `admin123`
2. View analytics at `/admin/dashboard`
3. Manage products at `/admin/products`
4. Update order statuses from dashboard
